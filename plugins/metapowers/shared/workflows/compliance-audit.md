# Compliance Audit

A comprehensive compliance check across legal, accessibility, and brand.

## Steps

1. `/legal:legal-audit <project>` — Assess current legal posture
2. `/legal:gdpr-compliance <project>` — Check GDPR compliance
3. `/legal:privacy-policy <project>` — Review privacy policy
4. `/legal:terms-of-service <project>` — Review terms of service
5. `/legal:cookie-policy <project>` — Review cookie policy
6. `/accessibility:scope <project>` — Define accessibility audit scope
7. `/accessibility:evaluate <project>` — Run WCAG checks
8. `/accessibility:report <project>` — Generate accessibility report
9. `/accessibility:remediate <project>` — Fix accessibility issues
10. `/accessibility:retest <project>` — Verify conformance
11. `/branding:audit <project>` — Check brand compliance
12. `/coaching:a11y <key-pages>` — Get accessibility coaching
13. `/metapowers:report` — Cross-domain compliance summary

## Tips
- Start with legal for the broadest risk assessment
- Run accessibility on your highest-traffic pages first
- Brand audit catches visual inconsistencies across touchpoints
