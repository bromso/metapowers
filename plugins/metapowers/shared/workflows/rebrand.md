# Rebrand

A complete rebranding workflow from research through rollout.

## Steps

1. `/research:discover <rebrand>` — Research why the rebrand is needed
2. `/research:define <rebrand>` — Define the rebrand objectives
3. `/branding:discover <brand>` — Audit current brand and competitors
4. `/branding:strategy <brand>` — Define new positioning and values
5. `/branding:verbal <brand>` — Develop new voice and messaging
6. `/branding:visual <brand>` — Create new visual identity
7. `/branding:guidelines <brand>` — Package into brand manual
8. `/coaching:copy <key-content>` — Review content against new brand
9. `/branding:audit <existing-materials>` — Score existing materials
10. `/marketing:strategy <rollout>` — Plan the rebrand rollout
11. `/metapowers:report` — Cross-domain summary

## Tips
- Place new brand assets in `.metapowers/branding/<brand>/assets/`
- Run brand audit on each major touchpoint before rollout
- The coaching:copy skill checks content against brand guidelines automatically
