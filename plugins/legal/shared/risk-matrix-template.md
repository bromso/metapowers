## Likelihood Scale

| Rating | Label | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Rare | Event may occur only in exceptional circumstances (less than 5% probability) |
| 2 | Unlikely | Event could occur but is not expected (5-20% probability) |
| 3 | Possible | Event could occur at some point (20-50% probability) |
| 4 | Likely | Event will probably occur in most circumstances (50-80% probability) |
| 5 | Almost Certain | Event is expected to occur in most circumstances (greater than 80% probability) |

---

## Impact Scale

| Rating | Label | Financial Impact | Operational Impact | Reputational Impact |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Negligible | Less than $10K | No disruption | No media attention |
| 2 | Minor | $10K - $100K | Minor disruption, resolved quickly | Limited local coverage |
| 3 | Moderate | $100K - $1M | Significant disruption, workarounds needed | Industry/trade coverage |
| 4 | Major | $1M - $10M | Major disruption, partial shutdown | National media coverage |
| 5 | Critical | Greater than $10M | Complete shutdown or existential threat | Sustained negative coverage, regulatory action |

---

## Risk Categories

| Category | Description | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Regulatory** | Non-compliance with laws and regulations | GDPR fines, SEC violations, environmental non-compliance |
| **Contractual** | Risks arising from contractual obligations | Breach of contract claims, unfavorable terms, warranty exposure |
| **Intellectual Property** | Risks to or from IP assets | Patent infringement, trade secret misappropriation, trademark disputes |
| **Employment** | Workforce-related legal risks | Wrongful termination, discrimination claims, wage and hour disputes |
| **Data Privacy** | Risks related to personal data handling | Data breaches, unauthorized processing, cross-border transfer violations |
| **Litigation** | Exposure to lawsuits and legal proceedings | Product liability, class actions, shareholder suits |

---

## Mitigation Strategies

| Strategy | Description | When to Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Avoid** | Eliminate the risk by not engaging in the activity | Risk is unacceptable and the activity is not essential |
| **Transfer** | Shift the risk to a third party via insurance or indemnification | Risk is significant but can be covered by insurance or contractual allocation |
| **Mitigate** | Reduce the likelihood or impact through controls and processes | Risk is manageable with appropriate safeguards |
| **Accept** | Acknowledge the risk and monitor without active intervention | Risk is low or the cost of mitigation exceeds the potential impact |

### Risk Rating Thresholds

| Score Range | Rating | Action Required |
|---|---|---|
| 1 - 5 | **Low** | Monitor during regular review cycles |
| 6 - 10 | **Medium** | Assign owner, develop mitigation plan within 90 days |
| 11 - 16 | **High** | Escalate to leadership, implement mitigation within 30 days |
| 17 - 25 | **Critical** | Immediate executive attention, mitigation within 7 days |

*Note: Apply the Jurisdiction Modifier (1.0-1.5x) to the base score for jurisdictions with heightened regulatory activity, stricter enforcement, or higher penalty ranges.*

---

## Jurisdiction Modifier Guide

| Modifier | When to Apply |
|---|---|
| **1.0x** (Baseline) | Standard regulatory environment, moderate enforcement history |
| **1.1-1.2x** (Elevated) | Active regulatory body, recent enforcement actions in the sector |
| **1.3-1.4x** (High) | Strict regulatory regime, significant penalty ranges (e.g., GDPR in EU) |
| **1.5x** (Maximum) | Aggressive enforcement, extraterritorial reach, class action exposure (e.g., California privacy, EU competition law) |

---

## Review Schedule

| Risk Rating | Review Frequency |
|---|---|
| Critical | Monthly |
| High | Quarterly |
| Medium | Semi-annually |
| Low | Annually |

All risks should be re-evaluated when triggered by material business changes, regulatory developments, or incident occurrence.
