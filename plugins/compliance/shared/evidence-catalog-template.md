## Notes

- Evidence should be stored in a centralized, access-controlled location
- Retain evidence for the period required by each applicable regulation (typically 3-7 years)
- Ensure evidence is tamper-evident (checksums, immutable storage, or audit trails)
- Cross-reference with `control-mapping-template.md` to identify which frameworks each piece of evidence supports
