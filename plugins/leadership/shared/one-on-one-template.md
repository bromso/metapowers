# 1:1 Meeting Template

## Preparation (5 min before)
- Review their OKR progress
- Check on action items from last 1:1
- Note anything to recognize or discuss

## Agenda (30-60 min)

### Check-in (5 min)
- How are you doing? (genuinely)
- Energy level and engagement

### Their Topics (15-20 min)
- What's on your mind?
- Any blockers I can help with?
- What's going well?

### Progress & Goals (10 min)
- OKR/project status
- Are priorities clear?
- Do you have what you need?

### Growth & Development (10 min)
- Skills you're building
- Feedback (SBI: Situation, Behavior, Impact)
- Career aspirations check-in

### Action Items (5 min)
- Summarize commitments from both sides
- Set follow-ups

## After
- Send summary of action items
- Follow through on your commitments
- Note topics to revisit next time
