# CFR Model: Conversations, Feedback, Recognition

A continuous performance management framework that replaces annual reviews.

## Conversations
Regular check-ins (weekly or biweekly) focused on:
- Progress toward goals
- Blockers and support needed
- Career development
- Well-being and engagement

## Feedback
Structured input on performance:
- **Feedback** — What happened, impact, and what to change (backward-looking)
- **Feedforward** — What's needed to reach goals (forward-looking)
- Use the SBI model: Situation, Behavior, Impact
- Be specific, timely, and actionable

## Recognition
Acknowledge contributions:
- Public recognition for team wins
- Private recognition for individual growth
- Tie recognition to values and behaviors, not just outcomes
- Be specific about what was done well and why it matters
