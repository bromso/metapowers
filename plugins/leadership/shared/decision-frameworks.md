# Decision-Making Frameworks

## When to Use Each Mode

| Mode | When | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| **Decide alone** | You have expertise, urgency is high, impact is low | Choosing a meeting time, minor tooling decisions |
| **Consult then decide** | Multiple perspectives needed, you own the outcome | Architecture decisions, hiring choices |
| **Delegate** | Team member has expertise, it's a growth opportunity | Feature design, sprint planning details |
| **Consensus** | High impact, needs buy-in from everyone | Team values, major process changes |

## RAPID Framework
- **Recommend:** Who proposes the solution?
- **Agree:** Who must agree before it proceeds?
- **Perform:** Who implements the decision?
- **Input:** Who provides information to inform the decision?
- **Decide:** Who has the final call?
