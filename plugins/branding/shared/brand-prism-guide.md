# Kapferer's Brand Identity Prism

A framework for evaluating brand consistency across six interconnected facets. Used by the audit skill to score brand compliance.

## The Six Facets

### 1. Physique
The tangible, visual elements — logo, colors, typography, imagery style, packaging. What the brand looks like.

**Audit checks:** Logo usage correct? Colors within palette? Typography consistent? Imagery matches style guide?

### 2. Personality
The brand's character traits and voice. If the brand were a person, how would it speak and behave?

**Audit checks:** Tone of voice consistent? Language style matches guidelines? Personality traits reflected in copy?

### 3. Culture
The values and principles the brand stands for. Its heritage, beliefs, and what drives it.

**Audit checks:** Values communicated? Brand story consistent? Cultural positioning maintained?

### 4. Relationship
How the brand interacts with its audience. The type of connection it builds.

**Audit checks:** Communication style appropriate? Customer touchpoints consistent? Relationship tone maintained?

### 5. Reflection
The idealized image of the target audience. Who the brand's customer aspires to be.

**Audit checks:** Target audience correctly addressed? Aspirational messaging aligned? Demographic/psychographic fit?

### 6. Self-Image
How customers see themselves when using the brand. The internal mirror.

**Audit checks:** User experience matches brand promise? Self-identification cues present? Emotional resonance maintained?

## Scoring

Each facet is scored 1-5:
- **5:** Fully compliant — all elements align perfectly
- **4:** Mostly compliant — minor inconsistencies
- **3:** Partially compliant — noticeable deviations
- **2:** Weakly compliant — significant misalignment
- **1:** Non-compliant — contradicts brand guidelines

**Overall score:** Average of all 6 facets (max 30, percentage out of 100%)
