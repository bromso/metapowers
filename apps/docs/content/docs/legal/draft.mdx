## Purpose

The Draft phase produces legal documents based on assessment findings. It covers the most common document types — from customer-facing terms to internal policies and commercial agreements.

## Skills

### Terms of Service

```
/legal:terms-of-service <topic>
```

Generates comprehensive terms of service for digital products. Covers user obligations, intellectual property, liability limitations, and dispute resolution.

**Output:** Complete terms of service document → `.legal/<topic>/01-draft.md`

### Privacy Policy

```
/legal:privacy-policy <topic>
```

Creates a privacy policy covering data collection, processing, sharing, retention, and user rights. Adapts to applicable privacy regulations.

**Output:** Privacy policy with data practices documentation → `.legal/<topic>/01-draft.md`

### NDA

```
/legal:nda <topic>
```

Drafts non-disclosure agreements — mutual or one-way. Defines confidential information scope, obligations, exclusions, term, and remedies.

**Output:** NDA document with defined scope and terms → `.legal/<topic>/01-draft.md`

### SaaS Agreement

```
/legal:saas-agreement <topic>
```

Creates a software-as-a-service agreement covering subscription terms, service levels, data handling, support obligations, and termination provisions.

**Output:** SaaS agreement with SLA and data terms → `.legal/<topic>/01-draft.md`

### Employment Agreement

```
/legal:employment-agreement <topic>
```

Drafts employment agreements covering role, compensation, benefits, IP assignment, non-compete/non-solicit, confidentiality, and termination.

**Output:** Employment agreement with IP and restrictive covenants → `.legal/<topic>/01-draft.md`

### Vendor Agreement

```
/legal:vendor-agreement <topic>
```

Creates vendor and supplier agreements covering scope of services, payment terms, warranties, indemnification, and liability caps.

**Output:** Vendor agreement with service terms and protections → `.legal/<topic>/01-draft.md`

### Open Source Policy

```
/legal:open-source-policy <topic>
```

Develops an internal policy for open source usage and contribution. Covers license classification, approval workflows, and compliance requirements.

**Output:** Open source policy with license matrix and workflows → `.legal/<topic>/01-draft.md`

### Cookie Policy

```
/legal:cookie-policy <topic>
```

Creates a cookie policy covering cookie categories, purposes, third-party cookies, consent mechanisms, and management instructions.

**Output:** Cookie policy with category breakdown → `.legal/<topic>/01-draft.md`
