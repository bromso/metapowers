## Purpose

The Govern phase maintains ongoing legal operations. It covers contract lifecycle management, compliance monitoring, regulatory change tracking, playbook maintenance, and team training.

## Skills

### Contract Tracker

```
/legal:contract-tracker <topic>
```

Sets up contract lifecycle tracking. Monitors renewal dates, obligation deadlines, key milestones, and expiration alerts across the contract portfolio.

**Output:** Contract tracking framework with renewal calendar → `.legal/<topic>/04-govern.md`

### Compliance Monitor

```
/legal:compliance-monitor <topic>
```

Establishes ongoing compliance monitoring processes. Defines monitoring schedules, audit checklists, reporting cadences, and escalation procedures.

**Output:** Compliance monitoring framework with audit schedule → `.legal/<topic>/04-govern.md`

### Regulatory Updates

```
/legal:regulatory-updates <topic>
```

Tracks regulatory changes relevant to the organization. Monitors legislative developments, assesses impact, and recommends necessary policy updates.

**Output:** Regulatory change tracker with impact assessments → `.legal/<topic>/04-govern.md`

### Legal Playbook

```
/legal:legal-playbook <topic>
```

Creates and maintains legal operations playbooks. Documents standard processes, approval workflows, escalation paths, and decision-making frameworks.

**Output:** Legal operations playbook with process documentation → `.legal/<topic>/04-govern.md`

### Training Plan

```
/legal:training-plan <topic>
```

Develops legal awareness training for the organization. Covers compliance training, contract management basics, IP awareness, and data protection practices.

**Output:** Training plan with modules, schedules, and assessment criteria → `.legal/<topic>/04-govern.md`

## After Govern

The Legal Lifecycle is complete. Use [Utility Skills](/docs/legal/utilities) for ongoing legal research, clause management, and knowledge building.
