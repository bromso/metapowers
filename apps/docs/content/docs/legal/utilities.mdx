## Purpose

Utility skills work across all Legal Lifecycle phases. They have no prerequisites and can be run anytime — for research, clause reuse, decision recording, or terminology clarification.

## Skills

### Legal Research

```
/legal:legal-research <topic>
```

Researches legal precedents, regulatory frameworks, and industry standards. Provides structured analysis of relevant legal authorities and their applicability.

**Output:** Research memo with precedent analysis and regulatory summary → `.legal/<topic>/legal-research.md`

### Clause Library

```
/legal:clause-library <topic>
```

Maintains a reusable library of legal clauses organized by category. Provides standard, favorable, and fallback versions of common contract provisions.

**Output:** Clause collection with variations and usage guidance → `.legal/<topic>/clause-library.md`

### Decision Memo

```
/legal:decision-memo <topic>
```

Documents legal decisions and their rationale. Records the issue, options considered, analysis, decision reached, and any conditions or follow-up actions.

**Output:** Decision memo with analysis and rationale documentation

### Legal Glossary

```
/legal:legal-glossary <topic>
```

Generates plain-language glossaries of legal terms for non-legal stakeholders. Defines key terms, explains implications, and provides practical examples.

**Output:** Glossary with definitions, context, and examples
