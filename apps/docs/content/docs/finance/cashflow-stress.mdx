## Purpose

The Cash Flow Stress utility models liquidity risk by simulating adverse cash flow scenarios — delayed collections, accelerated payments, revenue shortfalls — to determine cash runway and identify potential liquidity crises.

## Usage

```
/finance:cashflow-stress <project>
```

## What It Does

1. Models baseline cash flow projections
2. Applies stress scenarios (revenue drop, collection delays, cost spikes)
3. Calculates cash runway under each stress scenario
4. Identifies the point at which cash reserves are exhausted
5. Recommends liquidity buffers and contingency measures

## Output

- Stressed cash flow projections by scenario
- Cash runway analysis (months of coverage)
- Liquidity risk assessment
- Recommended cash reserve levels
