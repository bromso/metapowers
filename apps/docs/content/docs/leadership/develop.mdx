## Purpose

The Develop phase focuses on growing your people — setting team OKRs, creating individual development plans, and establishing a feedback cadence.

## Usage

```
/leadership:develop <team-name>
```

## Prerequisites

Requires `.metapowers/leadership/<team>/03-build.md`. Use `--skip-checks` to bypass.

## What It Does

1. Sets 2-3 team-level OKRs with measurable key results
2. Creates individual growth plans per team member
3. Defines the feedback cadence (weekly 1:1s, monthly reviews, quarterly OKRs)
4. Documents your support commitments
5. Writes to `.metapowers/leadership/<team>/04-develop.md`

## Output

Creates `.metapowers/leadership/<team>/04-develop.md` containing team OKRs, individual growth plans, feedback cadence, and support commitments.

## Next Phase

After Develop, proceed to [Sustain](/docs/leadership/sustain) to establish ongoing rituals.
