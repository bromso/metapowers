## Purpose

The Sustain phase establishes the recurring rituals and processes that keep your team healthy, aligned, and growing over time.

## Usage

```
/leadership:sustain <team-name>
```

## Prerequisites

Requires `.leadership/<team>/04-develop.md`. Use `--skip-checks` to bypass.

## What It Does

1. Defines recurring rituals (weekly 1:1s, biweekly retros, monthly reviews, quarterly OKRs)
2. Creates a leader's playbook for common situations
3. Establishes team health metrics and warning signs
4. Defines renewal triggers — when to revisit earlier phases
5. Writes to `.leadership/<team>/05-sustain.md`

## Output

Creates `.leadership/<team>/05-sustain.md` containing ritual calendar, leader's playbook, health metrics, and renewal triggers.

## After Phases

Use the [day-to-day tools](/docs/leadership#day-to-day-tools) for ongoing leadership work.
