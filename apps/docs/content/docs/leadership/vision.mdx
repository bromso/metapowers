## Purpose

The Vision phase defines what your team stands for — its purpose, values, and cultural norms. This becomes the foundation for every leadership decision.

## Usage

```
/leadership:vision <team-name>
```

## Prerequisites

Requires `.metapowers/leadership/<team>/01-assess.md` from the Assess phase. Use `--skip-checks` to bypass.

## What It Does

1. Defines team purpose and connection to organizational mission
2. Establishes 3-5 core values with behavioral examples
3. Creates a culture blueprint (decision-making, communication, conflict norms)
4. Paints a success vision for 6 months out
5. Writes to `.metapowers/leadership/<team>/02-vision.md`

## Output

Creates `.metapowers/leadership/<team>/02-vision.md` containing team purpose, values, culture blueprint, and success vision.

## Next Phase

After Vision, proceed to [Build](/docs/leadership/build) to structure the team.
