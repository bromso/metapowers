## Purpose

The Chart of Accounts utility designs and maintains the hierarchical account structure used to categorize all financial transactions. A well-structured chart of accounts is the foundation of accurate bookkeeping.

## Usage

```
/bookkeeping:chart-of-accounts <project>
```

## What It Does

1. Designs an account hierarchy tailored to the business type
2. Assigns account numbers following standard numbering conventions
3. Groups accounts into categories (assets, liabilities, equity, revenue, expenses)
4. Defines sub-accounts for granular tracking
5. Validates the structure for completeness and consistency

## Output

- Complete chart of accounts with account numbers and descriptions
- Category hierarchy diagram
- Account usage guidelines
